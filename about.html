First Choice Group Inc.

First Choice Group, Inc. is an award winning recruiting firm. Our structured recruitment methodology results in a proven formula for success. At FCG we research, screen, and deliver thoroughly qualified candidates, saving your staff considerable time and effort in the hiring process.

First Choice Group has expertise in the Manufacturing & Information Systems industries and focuses on recruitment and placement of CNC machining professionals, and Information Technology professionals.

Our goal at First Choice Group is not only to add value beyond the human resource discipline, but also to help your organization grow through the acquisition of difference-making talent and proven industry resources. We treat each new client as potential long-term partners in success.

Your business depends on people, and we’re in the business of identifying winners – the talented people to make your business thrive and grow.

Everyone loves to win, to come in first. At First Choice Group, we put you, the client first – above all other considerations.

You have my commitment that First Choice Group’s first objective is to ensure your company’s human capital requirements are met and exceeded.

We look forward to partnering with you and your team!

Founding Partner & President
