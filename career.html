First Choice Group has expertise in the Manufacturing industry and focuses on recruitment and placement of CNC machining professionals.

As such, First Choice Group specializes in recruiting manufacturing professionals in the following job categories:

CNC Machine Operator
  • Ability to run most CNC machines effectively
  • Monitor CNC milling and turning machine to specifications on inspection sheets/prints
  • Possess full knowledge of most measuring equipment
  • Perform inspections and maintain tolerances according to specifications on prints
  • Proven ability to recognize and communicate problems with CNC machines
  • Work in a team setting by sharing responsibilities, such as running multiple CNC machines
Quality Control
  • Experience in preparing work to be accomplished by studying assembly instructions, blueprint specifications, and parts lists; gathering parts, subassemblies, tools, and materials
  • Ability to communicate and interact with customers
  • Proficiency in verifying specifications by measuring completed component
  • Experience in maintaining and keeping adequate inventories for parts and gages
  • Experience in keeping gages calibrated
  • Ability to document and complete production and quality forms
  • Proficiency in Controls and Instrumentation, and inspection tools
  • Experience in statistics & other Automotive Industry Action Group (AIAG) Core Tools [Production Part Approval Process (PPAP), Statistical Process Control (SPC), Measurement Systems Analysis (MSA), Failure Mode and Effects Analysis (FMEA), Advanced Product Quality Planning (APQP)]
  • Competent in reading CAD data
